  • Sport Law

Sport Law is very much an uncharted area of law in Nigeria, and the firm has a unique expertise in this specialized and extremely high value area of law. Having successfully negotiated a historic management agreement between Seye Ogunlewe (the fastest man in Nigeria in 2015) and Temple Management Company, the firm has gone on to represent a number of football clubs in various tiers of the Nigerian Football League and assisted them in both legal and business development capacity.
