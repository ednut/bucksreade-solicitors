Employment and labour is no longer approached from a casual perspective. With our services, we provide the much needed legal support for the human resources department of a company. We draft and review contracts of employment, service agreements, employment handbooks, and operating manuals.

Also, we advise on the implementation of redundancy or termination of employment thus making sure that an employer acts within the scope of its contracts and the law as applicable.
