Our corporate law practice is very extensive. It covers aspects concerning the organization, structure, and governance of a business including changes that occur within the business as influenced by changes in ownership, shareholding, funding, and perhaps the possible implication of a new or amended legislation relevant to an industry within which a company is operating. Far beyond the commencement of a company, we also understand that the survival and growth of any business thrives on continuous legal support and compliance with laws and regulations relevant to the operation of any given business.

Our services include:

  1. Business Restructuring
  2. Mergers and Acquisition
  3. Asset Acquisition
  4. Management Buy-out/Buy-in
  5. Compromise and Arrangement
  6. Shares transfer/acquisition
  7. Liquidation/Winding Up
  8. Insolvency
